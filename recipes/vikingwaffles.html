Viking Waffles (Original Recipe from Erik The Red)

How to Make Viking Waffles

This is a very simple recipe that takes less than an hour to complete in a standard kitchen.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour (1.5 cups)
  • Baking Powder (3.5 teaspoons)
  • Large Eggs (2)
  • White Sugar (2 tablespoon)
  • Milk (1.75 cups)
  • Melted Shortening (3 tablespoons)
  • Salt (1 tablespoon)
  • Vanilla Extract (1 teaspoon)

Steps

  1. Collect the above-referenced ingedients.
  2. Heat up the waffle iron (or use strong skillet with heel of boot for waffle indentations)
  3. Mix flour and baking soda together in a bowl
  4. Beat eggs and sugar into a separate bowl with your favorite axe or longsword until fluffy
  5. Add milk, melted shortening, salt, and vanilla and mix the flour into it
  6. Pour 2/3 cup batter onto the hot waffle iron.
  7. Close iron, cook until moisture evaporates and the waffle smells good
