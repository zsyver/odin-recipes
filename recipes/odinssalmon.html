Odin's Epic Salmon

How to make Epic Salmon (Odin's Way)

This is a very simple recipe that takes less than a a few hours to complete in a standard kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillets (1.5 lbs)
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce (0.5 cups)
  • Brown Sugar (0.33 cups)
  • Water (0.33 cups)
  • Vegetable Oil (0.25 cups)

Steps

  1. Collect the above-referenced ingedients.
  2. Lay out salmon fillets on the cooking pan or griddle to be 1 - 2 inches apart
  3. Mix together the soy sauce, brown sugar, and vegetable oil until the sugar has dissolved.
  4. Pour a little of the mixture on top of the salmon fillets immediately or soak the fillets in the mixture for 1 - 2 hours prior to cooking
  5. Preheat grill (or oven) on medium heat
  6. Cook salmon until fillets flake easily with a fork (6 - 8 minutes on medium heat)
  7. Serve Odin's Epic Salmon to a hungry Viking (tastes great with vegetables too!)
