True Norwegian Lefse

How to make lefse

This is a very simple recipe that takes less than a few hours to complete in a standard kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (10 lbs)
  • Butter (0.5 cups)
  • Heavy Cream (0.5 cups)
  • Salt (1 tablespoon)
  • White Sugar (1 tablespoon)
  • All-purpose Flour (2.5 cups)

Steps

  1. Collect the above-referenced ingedients.
  2. Place potatoes into a pot
  3. Pour water into the pot and heat until water boils
  4. Rice the potatoes into a large bowl
  5. Mix together the sugar, butter, cream, salt, and riced potatoes into a large bowl
  6. Stir flour into potato mix and make round-shaped pieces of dough (3 - 4 inch in diameter)
  7. Sprinkle flour onto a cloth and roll out the round-shaped dough into 1/8 inch thick patties
  8. Heat up griddle and cook lefse until brown blisters appear (approximately 1 minute per side)
  9. Remove lefse from griddle and enjoy!
