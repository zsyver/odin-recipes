Longboat Pancakes

How to make delicious Longboat Pancakes

This is a very simple recipe that even a Viking could follow. It takes less than a few hours to complete in a standard kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Milk (1.5 cups)
  • Eggs (3)
  • All-purpose Flour (1 cup)
  • Sugar (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (0.25 teaspoon)
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Collect the above-referenced ingedients.
  2. Combine milk and eggs, then add flour, sugar and salt together.
  3. Spray large skillet with cooking spray and heat it over medium-high heat
  4. Place approximately 0.25 cups of batter onto the skillet and cook until water evaporates
  5. Flip the patty and finish grilling it on the other side for a few seconds
  6. Remove the longboat pancake from the skillet. The longboat has completed the journey. It is now on dry land.
